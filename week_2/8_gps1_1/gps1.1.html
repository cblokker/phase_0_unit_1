Pair Programming Concepts

Emotional

  • Mentally prepare yourself for your pair programming session
  • Be sure to check in with your partner
  • Be empathetic to your partner

Technical

  • Decide who is the driver and who is the navigator
  • Driver - writes the code and communicates with navigator
    Navigator - keeps pair on track
  • Use collaborative tools, like google hangouts
